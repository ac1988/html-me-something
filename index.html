My Trip to Panama City Beach

The Best Parts of My Vacation

  • The beautiful beach
  • Dining at Barefoot Grill
  • Cruising through the bay on a double decker pontoon
  • Enjoying margaritas
  • Seeing the smile on my kids' faces
  • Spending quality time with my family

The beautiful beach

I have visited Florida many times. I lived in Orlando and my parents currently live just outside of Daytona Beach. This was the first time that I have had the pleasure of visiting a beach on the Gulf side. The sand was so clean that it squeaked when you walked on it. The water was such a beautiful shade of blue. This was unlike any beach that I have experienced in the past and I can't wait to go back one day!

Dining at Barefoot Grill

This restaurant was perfect in every way. It was located right on the beach, it had amazing food & drinks, and it even had a kiddie pool to keep my kids entertained so I could enjoy my meal for a change!

Cruising through the bay on a double decker pontoon

The third day that we were in Panama City Beach we rented a double decker pontoon and it was such a great way to experience all that Panama City Beach had to offer. While cruising through the bay, we even got to see dolphins! We also anchored the boat at Shell Island where we were able to enjoy the two water slides off the back of the boat!

Enjoying margaritas

You can't have a beach vacation without margaritas! I was able to showcase some of my startending skills when making these delicious pineapple cadillac margaritas. Mix up a little 1800 Tequila, sour mix, pineapple juice, Grand Marnier, and blue curacao...squeeze in a lime and you have yourself a vaction-worthy cocktail!

Seeing the smile on my kids' faces

I have an 11 year old son, a 2 1/2 year old girl, and a 10 month old girl. They all really enjoyed this vacation! This was the first time at the beach for the two girls. My 2 1/2 year old was OBSESSED with playing in the sand! Honestly, seeing these kids smile throughout this trip was probably the absolute best part for me! Their happiness means the world to me!

Spending quality time with my family

Both of my brothers live here in St. Louis, but my parents live in Florida, so it is rare for all of us to get together at once. This vacation was so special because we got to have some quality bonding time since we were all staying together in the condo off of the beach. We had such a fabulous time just relaxing and enjoying eachother's company!
